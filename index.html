About CoHeart & HerEcho

Our Story

CoHeart, registered in 2023, stands for "Collaborative with Heartful Individuals and Organizations." We believe in the power of community-driven change and compassionate action.

HerEcho is our flagship women's empowerment initiative, designed to amplify women's voices across all age groups - from girl children to senior citizens.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive and compassionate society where women and communities thrive with dignity, equal opportunity, and holistic well-being.

Our Objectives

  • Ensure every girl child receives education, dignity, and protection
  • Empower youth, especially young women, with skills and opportunities
  • Strengthen mental well-being and emotional resilience across communities
  • Create safe, nurturing spaces for women of all ages
  • Promote community-centered care systems for senior citizens

Our Approach

Community-Based

Built with families, women's groups, and local leaders

Inclusive

Ensuring no voice, age, or background is left behind

Integrated

Weaving education, health, livelihood, and care together

Sustainable

Creating models communities can sustain themselves

Our Initiatives in Detail

  • Bridge learning support for disadvantaged girls
  • Scholarships and digital/STEM learning opportunities

  • Vocational training aligned with market needs
  • Career counseling and placement support
  • Entrepreneurship guidance
  • Health camps and nutrition awareness

  • Advocacy for dignified ageing
  • Community-Centered Care Units
